What is Stardew Valley?

Stardew Valley is a pixel art farming game. Your character takes over your grandfather's old farm, after feeling burnt out from life in the big city. You meet several interesting townsfolk and even a few potential love interests. Your main goal of the game is to farm, develop relationships, explore, and just live your dream farm life. It has unlimited replayability and playing it on PC gives you the opportunity to add mods which expands the world even more. It is definitely a game worth checking out.
